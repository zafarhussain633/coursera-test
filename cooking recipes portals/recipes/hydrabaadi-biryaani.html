hydrabaadi biryaani

biryaani is indian dish which is famous from mughal time in india.

Basic Ingridents
  • basmati rice
  • chicke/mutton
  • cooking oil
  • coriender leafs
  • ginger and Garlic
  • spices(garam masala,turmanic,chilli,etc)
  • biryaani masal(optional)
  • curd and lemon
  • salt
Steps to Cook
  1. Marination: marinate your chicke/mutton with curd and spices for 30 min
  2. boiling the rice boil the rice with(oil, salt,bayleafs,spices)
  3. layering when your rice is 80% cooked then layer of marinate chicken and rice
  4. Adding flavourafter the layering add brown onions , food color , coriender leafs, green chilli on the top of layer
  5. and leave it on low flame for 15 min
